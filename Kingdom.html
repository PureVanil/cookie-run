Cookie Run: Kingdom

The history of Cookie Run: Kingdom.
It was first released in the Winter of 2020, then released globally on January 19, 2021, with a release of 37 cookies. Currently there are 88 playable cookies. During a battle, you can put up to 5 cookies in a team. The first update after launch was on February 5th, releasing a new cookie, Kumiho Cookie and a new epic treasure, The Sacred Pomegranate Branch. There are also new game mechanics, decor theme, and a new event. The decor theme being the Lunar New Year, the new mechanic being Dark Mode, which is like World Exploration, but the levels are harder to beat, and the new event being Kumiho Cookie's Lucky Pouch and Cookies of Darkness. The most recent update was a collab with BTS, releasing the BTS cookies, Schwarzwalder, and Macaron Cookie, who was released a few days after the update. The magic candy for Werewolf Cookie also gets released along with Macaron Cookie. New costumes also gets released that are related to B.A.D 4. The cookies are Poison Mushroom Cookie(MUSHY P.), Schwarzwalder(T.N.T), Licorice Cookie(ZZ Skull), and Red Velvet Cookie(Hellhoud).
