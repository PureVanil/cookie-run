Cookie Run: Ovenbreak

The history of Cookie Run: Ovenbreak.
There were three Ovenbreak games that were made through the years by Devsisters. Ovenbreak was the first game to be released by Devsisters on June 15, 2009. Ovenbreak can referred to many other names like, Ovenbreak Lite, Ovenbreak: Gingerbrave Run, Ovenbreak Infinity, or Ovenbreak Skittles. It was an endless arcade style game and has multiple different modes. On December 11, 2012, the devs released a sequel to Ovenbreak, Ovenbreak 2. Devsisters then release Cookie Run: Ovenbreak on September 26, 2016 in Canada, Sweden, Netherlands, Philippines, Hong Kong, and Australia. They has a pre-registration event that is no longer active. On October 27 of 2016, the game released globally except China. It was the first game that they have released since the launch on LINE Cookie Run on January 2014, and marks the departure of the usage of LINE and Kakao, which were instant messaging platforms.
